Vision

HelpHud seeks to become the top online community service platform.

Mission

HelpHud will provide volunteers the means to seek, register, and communicate with community service providers.

Features

Volunteers

Discover Local Projects in the Area
Select Which Tasks To Complete
Offer Feedback to Providers.

Providers

Access a Free Market of Volunteers
Break Larger Projects into Smaller Tasks
Use Feedback to Improve Future Projects
